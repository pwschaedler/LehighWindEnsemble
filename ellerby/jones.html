Dr. Graham O. Jones MBE

After a glittering career in military music as the Senior Director of the Household Division Lieutenant Colonel Graham Jones MBE retired from military service to embark on a career as a professional conductor and lecturer.

Since embracing his new career Graham has travelled extensively guests conducting bands and orchestras in USA, Switzerland, Poland, Sweden, Finland, Latvia, Italy, UK and Ireland. During 2016 Graham will be conducting in Mexico and Russia amongst an ever growing international schedule.

With his orchestra, the British Imperial Orchestra, he has conducted at major events including St Petersburg Ball, Congress of Vienna Ball, Russian Summer Ball and Freedom to Dance.

Graham has just been appointed visiting resident conducting lecturer for Troy University in Alabama, USA and continues to enjoy guest lecturing for both Cunard and P&O shipping companies as well enjoying a growing reputation as an after dinner speaker.
